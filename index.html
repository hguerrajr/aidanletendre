Aidan LeTendre is a New York-based filmmaker from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

From the first time he picked up a camera to his thesis film at New York University's Tisch School of the Arts, Aidan has explored various styles and genres of filmmaking, garnering praise from numerous film festivals worldwide.

Co-founder of Praetorian Pictures and Film School Rentals, Aidan is dedicated to pursuing excellence in the film industry while balancing his athletic pursuits as a triathlete, weightlifter, and avid golfer.
